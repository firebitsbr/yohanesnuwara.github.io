About

I am a geophysicist. I was awarded the Most Outstanding Student in the department of geophysical engineering, in 2018. Some of my greatest achievements are three campus awards from the Rector of Bandung Institute of Technology in 2018 and 2019 and selected as best 9 teams in Equinor's (Statoil) Minus CO2 Challenge Competition in 2019. A variety kinds of roles I have been involved, such as coordinator of lecture assistant, leader in organization, and chief editor in a professional magazine. My latest specialty is in the research of Carbon Capture and Storage, and my major interest is in the field of reservoir characterization, modeling, and simulation for the oil and gas industry. I am particularly interested in the field of numerical modelling, computational simulations, and machine learning. Side hobby is writing about science and technology for Towards Data Science, renowned Medium publication in science and technology.
